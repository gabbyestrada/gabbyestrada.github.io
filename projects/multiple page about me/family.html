My hometown

I was born and am being raised in Newburgh, New york. Newburgh is famous for all the history that took place here in the past, including the beautiful architecture. Today Newburgh is known, but not for such great things. It's actually quite sad to see my hometown in the rut that it's in, however Newburgh does have some little things that are completely overlooked way too often. In my opinion, we have the best veiws of the the Hudson River in all of New York. Especially by the waterfront, which happened to be my favorite place to visit when I was small. The street art here is also overlooked so often that I'm actually a bit suprised. There are colorful murals in some little parts of Newburgh that I just find completely beautiful.
