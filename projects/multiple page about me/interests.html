My Interests

  • I love art - all art, any art - new or old! One of my favorite artist is Vincent van Gogh. He's really old and it may sound cliche that I like him but I think it's mostly just his story and the fact that he kept painting despite what everyone in his time thought of him.

  • I love to show off my creativity. There are some things that I refuse to show the light of day to, but when I do want to, I could just sit and draw for hours if you let me. I practice my skills all the time with small doodles and sketches.

  • I also like making collages. I starting doing this when I couldn't find posters that were as diverse as my interests. So I decided to print, cut and glue a bunch of pictures together. They're hanging all over my room and even on my binders, an I have to admit they're pretty cool looking ^ - ^

  • My favorite music artist are The Neighbourhood, 5 Seconds of Summer, Ed Sheeran, Ariana Grande, an many, many more!
