Dear Mr. Talmud,

During some recent reflection I have come to remember the factors that has lead me to the success in which I hold. You, Mr. Talmud, being one of them. Firstly, allow me to reintroduce myself. My name is Albert Einstein and I am currently housed in Princeton, New Jersey in the United States of America, where I have been recently named a citizen.(Muhammad 1) I am a Jewish man and was born in Ulm, Germany and raised by my parents Herman and Pauline Einstein, whom I presume you remember. Sincerly, Albert Einstein, an old family friend.
